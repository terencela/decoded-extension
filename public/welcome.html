Your LinkedIn feed just got honest.

Decoded reads every post on linkedin.com and tells you what it actually means. Behavior archetype, AI score, plain-English translation. Zero clicks needed.

How to use it

1
Open LinkedIn. Decoded scans posts in your feed automatically. Hover any post to see its AI score and behavior badge.
2
Click "Decode" — or press Ctrl/⌘ + Shift + D. Get a plain-English translation of what the post is actually doing, with highlighted phrases.
3
Share the receipt. Generate a 1200×630 share card and paste it into X, Stories, or Slack.
4
Want it always-on? Open the popup and turn on "Always-on inline mode" to skip the hover.
